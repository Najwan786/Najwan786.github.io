PRO CLUBS & VOLTA FOOTBALL

PRO CLUBS

Pro Clubs in FIFA 23 is a multiplayer mode where players create and customize their own virtual pro and join or form clubs with friends. Teams compete in online matches, with each player controlling their own unique pro, aiming to rise through the ranks and earn promotions.

VOLTA FOOTBALL

Football in FIFA 23 focuses on small-sided, street-style football matches played in diverse, urban locations around the world. It emphasizes flair, creativity, and fast-paced gameplay, offering a variety of modes and customization options for players to create and style their own characters and teams.
